Welcome Spellcasters! This page will provide a basic Overview of how magic works in Genesys.

To use any magic you must have at least 1 rank in the skill.

Before casting a spell, there are options that you may add on to the spell you are using, each additional effect will add some levels of difficulty, after everything is added you may not exceed a Formidable check (5 difficulty)
